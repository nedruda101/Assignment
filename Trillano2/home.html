Legendary Sannin

Jiraiya

Jiraiya is one of the Legendary Sannin, known as the "Toad Sage." He was a skilled ninja and a mentor to Naruto Uzumaki.

Orochimaru

Orochimaru, the "Snake Sage," is a powerful and cunning ninja with a fascination for immortality and forbidden jutsu.

Tsunade

Tsunade, the "Slug Sage," is known for her incredible strength and healing abilities. She later becomes the Fifth Hokage.
